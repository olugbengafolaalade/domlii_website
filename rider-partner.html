Frequently asked questions

Here’s the answers for
your questions

What is delivering with Domlii like?

Delivering with Domlii is great! You can earn money with every delivery and every mile you drive while on a flexible schedule. Apply today and be your own boss with Domlii for Drivers!

How does delivering with Domlii work?

After a Domlii diner places an order, our drivers can accept it and earn money by picking up and delivering the order! Our drivers earn money with every delivery and every mile they drive while keeping 100% of their tips!

Why should I become a Domlii driver?

Here are just a few reasons why drivers chose to partner with Domlii:

  • Quick application & flexible schedule

  • Earn money with every delivery and every mile you drive

  • Keep 100% of your tips, No exceptions

  • Make money on your terms—no passengers, no small talk

  • No resume, interview or delivery experience required

  • Contact-free delivery and access to order personal protective equipment for your health & safety.

When can I start making deliveries as a new driver?

Once you are verified to partner with Domlii, you can start delivering immediately with any insulated delivery bag. Just download the Domlii for Drivers app and hit the road!

What is contact free delivery?

Contact-free delivery lets diners request the order be left at their door, in their lobby, or another place at the delivery location. Due to social distancing, Domlii diners are defaulted into the contact-free delivery option. Even if they don’t select this option directly, many of them are leaving clear instructions in the delivery notes.
