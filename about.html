How Domlii Began: Sharing Afro-Caribbean Heritage Through Food

DOMLII INC. is a platform that connects Afro-Caribbean consumers with their favorite restaurants in Toronto. Domlii builds products and services to help businesses management innovation , grow, customers satisfaction. Domlii uses his platform to build relationship between Afro-Caribbean restaurant and their consumers or communities. Exposing merchants to thrive in the convenience economy and providing work that empowers the community.

Domlii which comes from the name etymologically called "dô mi li" which means "food" in the Bambara language coming from present-day Mali or in Dioula (language spoken in Côte d'Ivoire). Domlii Inc. founded on August 05, 2020, in Toronto, Canada. vision to promote Afro-Caribbean culture to the world.  Domlii's mission is to help small and medium-sized businesses gain more visibility and at the same time increase their revenues.

This project will facilitate the consumption of products from Africa and the Caribbean, will allow us to be the major home delivery of Afro-Caribbean recipes to Canadian society without excluding other communities.  This project will consist of the well-being of Afro-Carribean circle and a major contribution to Canada economy in that industry. Showcasing Afro Caribbean restaurants, make it easier for customers to access and feel closer to home made dishes. Bringing memories at an affordable cost. Improving employment in the situation of the Afro-Caribbean circle and why not put ourselves at the global level by showing or prioritizing the value of the economy of our culture.
